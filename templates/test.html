Our Identity

OUR LADY OF HARVEST Your Mother and Protector

Congregation of the Daughters of Mary (DM) is a missionary congregation of Pontifical Right according to Canon Law CCEO 505(2). It is founded by Monsignor Joseph Kuzhinjalil, a zealous missionary priest of the Archdiocese of Changanassery, with the Paternal Blessings of the Servant of God and Architect of the Reunion Movement Archbishop Mar Ivanios in Syro Malankara Catholic Church on 8th May 1938 at Marthandom, in Kanyakumari District. The primary mission of the congregation is to bring Good News to the poor and neglected, concentrating families for their integrated development as well as to announce the Gospel of Christ to the end of the world, to foster unity.

The Congregation of the Daughters of Mary is dedicated to the Blessed Virgin Mary, the Lady of the Harvest. Its Motto is “That All May Be One”(Jn:17/21).It is governed by Codex Canonum Ecclesiorum Orientalium (CCEO ), Decrees and decisions of the Holy Apostolic See, by its Constitution and by the Directory, by decisions of Synaxis and by Superiors in accordance with the Gospel, in the Spirit of the Church and the Charism of the Congregation.

At present there are 6 provinces serving in all the Eparchies of the Syro Malankara Church and 56 dioceses in different parts of the world. The congregation has extended its service to 4 Continents namely, Asia, Europe, North America and Africa.
